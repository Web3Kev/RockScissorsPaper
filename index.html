How to Play

1. Make a thumbs up gesture to start the game.

2. When the countdown starts, prepare your move:

  • Fist 👊 for Rock
  • Victory ✌️ for Scissors
  • Open Palm 🖐️ for Paper

3. The result will be displayed after the countdown.

4. Make a thumbs up gesture to play again.
